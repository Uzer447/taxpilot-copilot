# TaxPilot Copilot

**Review. Validate. File with Confidence.**

Filing taxes shouldn't be a guessing game. TaxPilot Copilot is a premium, AI-powered financial dashboard that acts as your personal tax reviewer directly on the Indian Income Tax portal. It ensures your returns are accurate, optimized, and error-free before you hit submit.

## 🌟 Key Features

### 🛡️ Intelligent Document Validation
Upload your Form 16 or Salary Slips directly into the extension. TaxPilot intelligently cross-references the fields on the income tax portal against your official documents, instantly flagging any inconsistencies.

### 📚 Built-in Tax Knowledge Base
Stop searching the web for tax laws. TaxPilot proactively applies over 60 curated Indian tax rules to the current page, ensuring you are claiming the right deductions and avoiding common pitfalls.

### 💯 Filing Health Score
Get a clear, confidence-backed health score (0-100) for your current filing page. Instantly see a breakdown of validated fields, warnings, and critical issues that need your attention.

### 🔍 Field-by-Field Breakdown
Understand exactly what every textbox and dropdown means. The AI reads your screen and explains complex tax jargon in plain language, directly relevant to the numbers you are looking at.

### 🎨 Premium Financial Dashboard
Enjoy a professional, light-themed (or dark mode) interface that feels like premium financial software. It features a persistent tab navigation, step-by-step loading timelines, and expandable recommendation cards.

### 🔒 Privacy First
Your data remains secure. Documents are processed entirely in-memory and are never permanently stored, logged, or saved. They are instantly deleted the moment your session ends.

---

*Note: TaxPilot Copilot is an AI assistant designed to aid in the tax filing process. It does not replace professional financial advice.*
