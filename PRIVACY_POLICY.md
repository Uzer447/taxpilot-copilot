# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** December 10, 2024

Thank you for using TaxPilot Copilot ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you use the TaxPilot Copilot Chrome extension.

## 1. Data Collection & Usage

### A. Document Uploads (Form 16 / Salary Slips)
When you upload documents to TaxPilot Copilot for personalized validation:
- Documents are processed entirely in-memory on our secure servers.
- **We do not permanently store, log, or save your documents.**
- As soon as your session ends or you remove a document from the extension, it is instantly deleted from memory.

### B. Page Analysis (Screenshots & DOM Data)
When you click "Review This Page" or "Explain This Page":
- The extension captures a screenshot and extracts the text of the currently active tab.
- This data is sent securely (via HTTPS) to our backend for analysis using large language models.
- **We do not save or log these screenshots or extracted text.** They are used solely to generate the immediate review response and are discarded immediately after processing.

## 2. Third-Party Services

TaxPilot Copilot uses third-party Large Language Models (LLMs), such as Google Gemini, to analyze your data. 
- We do not use your personal tax data or uploaded documents to train our models.
- Data sent to these third-party APIs is governed by their respective privacy policies and is not used to train their foundational models when accessed via enterprise APIs.

## 3. Data Storage

- **Local Storage:** The extension uses Chrome's `chrome.storage.local` to store non-sensitive preferences (e.g., dark mode settings, session IDs). This data remains on your local device.
- **Server Storage:** We do not maintain a database of user accounts, tax information, or uploaded files. Our backend is entirely stateless between sessions.

## 4. Your Rights

Because we do not store your personal data, there is no account to delete or personal data to request. You have full control to clear your local session at any time by simply closing the extension or clearing your browser's local storage.

## 5. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with a new "Effective Date" at the top of this document.

## 6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact the developer via the support link provided in the Chrome Web Store.
